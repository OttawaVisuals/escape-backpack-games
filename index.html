How it works
01

Pick up the backpack

Borrow the backpack, take it home, gather some friends and get started.

02

Unpack and begin

Start the timer in the hint companion, open the first lock, and follow the trail of clues from one lock to the next. Use hints if you're stuck

03

Escape — and pass it on

Crack the final lock, reset the props using the checklist, and hand the backpack to the next curious friend.

The Hiking Backpack

Live

You are going on a hiking trip with your daughter Juno. While planning the trip, she asks you to secure her favourite plushy in the “special backpack”. You oblige and secure the plushy with a series of locks. The issue is, you have ADHD and know you won’t be able to remember all the combinations! But you planned ahead and left yourself clues to help find the correct combinations. Good luck! .

Medium difficulty 1–2 hrs 1–4 players Age 8+

What's inside

Hand-built props. Real locks. Paper you can hold.

Every backpack is assembled by hand. No app-only puzzles, no read-from-a-screen experience — the props are physical, and the trail through them is designed to feel like you've stumbled on someone else's adventure.

  • Physical locks and puzzles
  • Hand-made props and riddles
  • A digital hint companion for when you're stuck
  • A reset checklist so the next player starts fresh
